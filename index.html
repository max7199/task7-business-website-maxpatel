FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, reach out to us directly.

How long does a typical project take?
+
Project timelines vary based on scope. A standard website takes 2–4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 2–4 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase.
Do you offer post-launch support?
+
Yes! All our plans include post-launch support ranging from 1 to 12 months. We also offer ongoing retainer packages for continuous maintenance and improvements.
What technologies do you work with?
+
We work with React, Vue, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and more. We choose the best stack for each project based on your requirements, scalability needs, and budget.
Can I see the project progress in real-time?
+
Absolutely! We use Notion for project management and provide weekly progress updates. You'll also have access to a staging environment throughout the development process.
Do you handle design and development both?
+
Yes, we're a full-service agency. Our in-house designers and developers collaborate closely so there's no disconnect between your design vision and the final product.
What is your payment structure?
+
We typically follow a 40/40/20 payment model — 40% upfront, 40% at mid-project milestone, and 20% upon final delivery. Custom arrangements can be discussed.
